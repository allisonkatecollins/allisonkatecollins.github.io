About Me

I currently live in Nashville, TN.

I've always had varying career ideas and skills that I wanted to nurture, but for a time my interests seemed incongruous. I loved the human interest aspect of psychological research and counseling, the hard science and objectivity of veterinary medicine, the freedom and cultural diversity of teaching English abroad, and the freedom of personal expression through visual art. The only thing I was absolutely sure that I wanted to do was constantly learn new skills, either from personal research or from peers and mentors, lest my mind grow dull and restless. I enjoy being in school and building my knowledge base and problem solving skills.

Software development has turned out to be at the interesection of my varied interests and skills. I enjoy the challenge of solving puzzles and new problems. I also appreciate the experience and perspectives of those around me, and value their approaches to problem solving. My dream is to be a lifelong student, being at once proud of my own accomplishments and abilities as well as humbled by the talents and wisdom of others.
